We are DSBuilt.

DSBuilt has become one of Melbourne’s most trusted custom home builders. Whether you’re looking to customise and build your dream home or renovated your family home, with more than 20 year’s combined experience from small scale bathroom renovations to large scale town house developments DSBuilt is there to help every step of the way.


Founded by our Director, Doug, a qualified carpenter by trade who worked his way up to become a fully licensed Domestic Builder and start the business. Working closely with Doug is Chris, who brings extensive knowledge and experience in Construction Management. Chris joined the team just after it was founded as a Partner and Construction Manager making the team they are today. Doug & Chris believe the finer details, desire for transparency, trust and quality craftsmanship are the foundations for a flawless building experience which they pride themselves on delivering with each project.
